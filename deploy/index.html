The Texas Society of Neuroradiology

The Texas Society of Neuroradiology is dedicated to advancing the field of neuroradiology through education, collaboration, and innovation. Our mission is to support neuroradiologists across Texas by fostering professional growth, promoting high standards in clinical practice and research, and enhancing the quality of patient care in neurological imaging. Through conferences, networking opportunities, and advocacy, we strive to build a strong community committed to excellence in neuroradiology.
